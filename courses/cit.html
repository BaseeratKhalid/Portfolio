About Course

Essentials of Computing and Programming course is for newcomers to the field of IT. In this course, a variety of areas of Information technology have been included to help students understand their potential and pursue a particular discipline according to their inclination.

Eligibility

  • Minimum Matriculation with Science
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Able to read, write and understand the English language
  • Must have a laptop or PC

Course Information

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Classes: 2 Days a Week
  • Course Mode: Physical
  • Includes: Projects, Assignments, Certificates
  • Age limit: 14-20 years

Course Outcomes

The course content includes the following, Microsoft Office automation (MS Word, MS PowerPoint, and MS Excel), programming introduction with Python, and introduction to various Emerging IT Fields including Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Machine Learning and Artificial intelligence, Data Science effective use of trending AI tools. At the end of the course, students will be better prepared to kick start their IT career and know which fields excite them most to direct future study.
