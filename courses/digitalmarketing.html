About Course

This program will provide an introduction to digital marketing and all the major social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok and more to prepare you with the skills and tools needed to grow an engaging social community and run effective social media advertising

Eligibility

  • Minimum Matriculation or Equivalen
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Able to read, write and understand the English language
  • Must have a laptop or PC

Course Information

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Classes: 2 Days a Week
  • Course Mode: Physical
  • Includes: Projects, Assignments, Certificates
  • Age limit: 16-30 years

Course Outcomes

Understand the role of both digital and conventional marketing; distinguishing the intersection of online and offline strategies. Identify and present how the business can be scaled with an optimal marketing plan.Build and reach a target audience on various online platforms.Creating a professional freelance profile
