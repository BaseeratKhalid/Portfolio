About Course

Covering various techniques such as cold emailing, LinkedIn prospecting, cold calling, PPC advertising, data scraping, and more, you'll gain a deep understanding of generating organic leads and leveraging platforms like Facebook, Google, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Quora. Additionally, you'll explore lead capture, nurturing, and conversion strategies, as well as essential tools and lead magnet creation.

Eligibility

  • Minimum intermediate or Equivalen
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Able to read, write and understand the English language
  • Must have a laptop or PC

Course Information

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Classes: 2 Days a Week
  • Course Mode: Physical
  • Includes: Projects, Assignments, Certificates
  • Age limit: 18-35 years

Course Outcomes

By the end of this course, you'll possess a comprehensive skill set in lead generation. You'll be proficient in crafting compelling cold emails, utilizing LinkedIn to build targeted networks, conducting effective cold calls, managing PPC campaigns, leveraging data scraping techniques, and utilizing lead generation tools. You'll also understand how to generate organic leads through content marketing and SEO, and effectively generate leads from popular platforms.
