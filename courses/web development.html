About Course

In Web Designing Certificate, learn to start your future path towards developing interactive and professional websites, no matter how little technical knowledge and experience you currently have. Web Designing Certificate is designed to start your future path towards developing interactive and professional websites.

Eligibility

  • Minimum Intermediate
  • Basic computer knowledge
  • Able to read, write and understand the English language
  • Must have a laptop or PC

Course Information

  • Duration: 3 Months
  • Classes: 2 Days a Week
  • Course Mode: Physical
  • Includes: Projects, Assignments, Certificates
  • Age limit: 16-30 years

Course Outcomes

After completing this course, you will be able to design responsive websites, create animated UI, build dynamic pages using JavaScript, and launch your professional portfolio for internships and freelance work.
